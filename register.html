Our Chief Guest

Jagdeep Dhankhar

Governor of West Bengal

Jagdeep Dhankhar is an Indian politician and former leader of Bharatiya Janata Party. As of 2021, he is serving as the Governor of West Bengal. He is an advocate at the Supreme Court of India, and was a member of Lok Sabha from 1989 to 1991.

Our Guests of Honour

Sourav Ganguly

President of Board of Control for Cricket in India

Sourav Chandidas Ganguly, or Sourav Gangopadhyay, is an Indian cricket administrator, commentator and former national cricket team captain who is the 39th and current president of the Board of Control for Cricket in India. He is popularly honoured as Maharaja of Indian Cricket.

Srijit Mukherji

Indian film director

Srijit Mukherji is an Indian film director and screenwriter who predominantly works in Bengali cinema. His regular collaboration with veteran superstar Prosenjit Chatterjee brought him into the limelight.

Biman Bose

Indian politician, CPI(M)

Biman Bose is an Indian politician who was once state Secretary of the West Bengal Communist Party of India. He was later succeeded by his trusted aide, Surya Kanta Mishra, although he remained a Politburo member of the party. He is also Chairman of the Left Front committee of West Bengal.
